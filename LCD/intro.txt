Here is a step-by-step demonstration of how to build a thermometer using a TMP36 sensor, potentiometer, and 16x2 LCD display to display the temperature using Tinkercad:

Components required:

1. Arduino Uno board
2. TMP36 temperature sensor
3. 16x2 LCD display
4. Potentiometer (10K ohm)
5. Jumper wires
6. Breadboard
7. LED
8. Resisiter (1k ohm)
